The electrical subsystem provides power generation, distribution, control, and protection for the vehicle. It supplies energy from the battery and charging system to electronic components such as sensors, actuators, lighting, and control units. Through wiring, protection devices, and communication networks, it ensures safe, reliable, and coordinated operation of all electrical systems in the vehicle.
