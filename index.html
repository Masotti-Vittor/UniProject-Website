Summary Relamps

Relampinhão is a student project and competitive engineering team at the Universidade Tecnológica Federal do Paraná (UTFPR), especially active at the Curitiba campus. The team is part of a Shell Eco-Marathon effort, where university students design and build high-efficiency prototype vehicles to compete in energy-efficient driving challenges. In this project students organize into subsystems like aerodynamics and design, powertrain, structures, electronics, and marketing, all working together to build a competitive car. Relampinhão isn’t just a club — it’s a practical engineering project of extension, giving students real project experience in engineering design, teamwork, and competition on an international stage.
